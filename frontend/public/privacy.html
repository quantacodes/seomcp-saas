1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and authentication credentials
  • API Usage Data: API call counts, timestamps, and endpoints accessed
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Paddle (we do not store credit card details)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information for security and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the seomcp.dev service
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Monitor API usage and enforce rate limits
  • Send service updates and important notifications
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Detect and prevent fraud or security issues

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • API keys are hashed using SHA-256 before storage
  • Google OAuth tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database access is restricted and audited
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Paddle: Payment processing (PCI-compliant)
  • Cloudflare: CDN and DDoS protection
  • Google APIs: When you authorize access to Search Console/Analytics
  • Plausible Analytics: Privacy-focused website analytics (no personal data)

5. Your Google Data

When you connect Google Search Console or Google Analytics:

  • We request minimal necessary scopes (read-only access)
  • Your data flows through our servers but is not stored permanently
  • We cache responses temporarily (5-30 minutes) for performance
  • You can revoke access at any time through Google Account settings

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies:

  • Authentication: Session cookies for login (Clerk)
  • Analytics: Plausible (no personal data, GDPR-compliant)
  • We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account Data: Until you request deletion or close your account
  • Usage Logs: 90 days for operational purposes
  • Billing Records: 7 years for tax compliance
  • Cached API Responses: 5-30 minutes (automatically purged)

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion (right to be forgotten)
  • Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Object to automated decision-making

To exercise these rights, email us at: privacy@seomcp.dev

9. Children's Privacy
