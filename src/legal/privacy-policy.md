## 7. Data Retention

### 7.1 Active Accounts
- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Generated content: Retained per your content settings
- Usage analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 12 months
- API credentials: Deleted immediately upon revocation

### 7.2 Account Deletion
- **30 days after account deletion** - All personal data removed
- Generated content: Deleted unless you export it first
- Backup systems: Purged within 60 days
- Legal obligations: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

### 7.3 Subscription Cancellation
- Data retained until account deletion
- API credentials remain encrypted and accessible
- Export functionality available throughout retention period
